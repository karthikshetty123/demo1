WIFI

Colleges generally have open or secured WiFi networks with a range thats cover the whole campus area.These WiFi networks resembles to public WiFi networks like in Airports,Parks,Starbucks,KFC,McDonald's,etc.

These networks allow multiple users to get an even Internet access. By even, I mean all the users share equal amount of bandwidth and internet speeds.
